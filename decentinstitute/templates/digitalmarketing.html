Diploma in Digital Marketing

What You'll Learn

Gain in-depth knowledge of digital marketing techniques to enhance your skills and career opportunities.

  • Understand the basics of growth marketing and traction channels
  • Drill down on customer pain points and build user personas
  • Align marketing channels with personal preferences and skills
  • Leverage platforms like Twitter, Facebook, Reddit, and Meetup for research and sales outreach
  • Set up, manage, and analyze user stats & behaviors through Google Analytics
  • Drive sales through Reddit, ProductHunt, and HackerNews
  • Market effectively on Instagram, Snapchat, and YouTube
  • Build a comprehensive content marketing plan and content calendar
  • Learn video marketing, landing pages, SEO, and lead capture strategies
  • Manage keyword density and other metrics needed for ranking on Google
  • Design, plan, and start advertising on Google, Bing, and Facebook
  • Analyze & understand search engine marketing & social ad marketing metrics

Course Modules

Module 1: Introduction to Digital Marketing

Learn the fundamentals of digital marketing and identify different growth marketing channels.

Module 2: Customer Research and Persona Building

Learn how to research customer pain points and develop user personas.

Module 3: Social Media Marketing

Leverage platforms like Twitter, Facebook, Reddit, and Meetup for research, outreach, and sales.

Module 4: Google Analytics and User Behavior

Learn how to use Google Analytics to track user behavior and make data-driven marketing decisions.

Module 5: Press & Public Relations

Learn how to build relationships with the press, pitch stories, and get media coverage.

Module 6: Content Marketing & Blogging

Create and maintain a content marketing plan, and learn techniques to write blogs and articles.

Module 7: Video Marketing and YouTube

Learn how to produce, script, and market videos for platforms like YouTube.

Module 8: SEO and Lead Generation

Understand SEO best practices, keyword research, and how to generate leads with landing pages.

Mini Projects

Below are some mini projects to help you apply your learning from the course:

  • Build a Social Media Strategy for your brand
  • Conduct research on Twitter and Facebook for customer outreach
  • Set up Google Analytics for a website
  • Analyze user stats and create a report based on Google Analytics
  • Build a press list for your business
  • Pitch a story to a journalist
  • Build a content calendar and create blog posts
  • Produce and promote a YouTube video for marketing
  • Perform SEO keyword research for your website
  • Design and build a lead capture landing page
