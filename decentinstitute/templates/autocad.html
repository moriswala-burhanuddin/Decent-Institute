AutoCAD Course

What You'll Learn

Master AutoCAD drafting principles and techniques for mechanical, civil, architectural, electrical, and electronic applications.

  • Understanding AutoCAD Interface and Drafting Fundamentals
  • Coordinate Systems & Editing Tools
  • Navigation and Selection Techniques
  • Editing Techniques and Grip Editing
  • Manufacturability & Quality Assurance
  • Collaborative Features and Productivity Enhancements

Course Syllabus

Week 1: Introduction to AutoCAD

Overview, interface, drafting basics, and essential commands.

Week 2: Coordinate Systems & Editing

Absolute, relative, and polar coordinate systems, object snap, and dynamic input.

Week 3: Navigation & Selection Techniques

Zoom, pan, solid modeling, and selection methods.

Week 4: Editing & Grip Tools

Text editing, scaling, trimming, extending, and joining objects.

Week 5: Manufacturability Techniques

Bhatch, Pline, Pedit, chamfer, fillet, and spline tools.

Week 6: Quality Assurance

GD&T, dimensioning techniques, and multileader tools.

Week 7: Collaboration & Productivity

External referencing, plotting, layout management, and floating viewports.

Who Should Take This Course?

  • Mechanical, civil, electrical, and architectural drafters
  • Engineering and design professionals
  • Students looking to gain expertise in CAD software
  • Anyone interested in mastering 2D and 3D design in AutoCAD
