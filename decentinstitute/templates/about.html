ABOUT US:

INTRODUCTION

Our aim is to make one person in every family digitally literate, aligning with the Prime Minister's vision of "Digital India". DECENT COMPUTER EDUCATION focuses on providing computer training from basic to advanced level courses at affordable fees, enabling people to develop computer skills and become digitally empowered.

OUR AIM:

We aim to create digitally skilled individuals who can access online services, enhance their professional prospects, and contribute to the digital economy. Our goal is to make individuals digitally literate and help them succeed in the modern job market.

Courses Offered:

  • BASIC, INTERNET, CCC, CCA
  • TALLY, D.T.P., WEB DESIGNING
  • C, C++, AutoCAD, .NET, JAVA, VISUAL BASIC
  • SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT, HARDWARE & NETWORKING

What do STUDENTS get from DCE?

  • Government Recognition: Every person who passes the computer examination is issued a Government Authorized Certificate recognized by the Government of India.
  • Basic Computer Concepts Awareness: Students gain awareness of different computer benefits and fundamental knowledge.
  • Computer Accounting, Designing, Web Page Creation: Learn important skills related to computer accounting and design.
  • Ability to Seek Information Online: Students will learn how to search for information related to jobs, government services, and online banking.
  • Awareness of Online Services: Exposure to e-commerce, online transactions, communication channels like social media, and voice chatting.
