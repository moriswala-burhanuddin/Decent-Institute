Certificate in Desktop Publishing (DTP)

What You'll Learn

This course will help you master the fundamentals of DTP and graphic design software, including CorelDraw, Photoshop, and PageMaker. You will learn how to create stunning designs, edit photos, and handle multilingual publishing effectively, opening doors for a career in Graphic Designing and Desktop Publishing.

  • Master CorelDraw for vector design and logo creation
  • Learn Photoshop for photo editing, layers, and special effects
  • Use PageMaker for page layout and design
  • Work with typography, logo design, and corporate stationery
  • Understand how to work with multilingual scripts in Desktop Publishing
  • Create brochures, flyers, social media designs, and much more

Course Modules

Semester I

Logo Design

Learn how to design impactful logos that represent a brand effectively.

Corporate Stationery

Design business cards, letterheads, and other corporate materials.

Brand Identity

Understand the elements of brand identity and create cohesive branding.

Flyer Design

Create eye-catching flyers for marketing purposes using DTP tools.

Brochure Design

Design professional brochures for business promotions or events.

Infographic Design

Learn to design visually appealing infographics for clear communication.

Product Design

Create product packaging and labels that enhance visual appeal.

Character Design (2D/3D)

Design 2D and 3D characters for various media applications.

Image Editing Effects

Master techniques for applying image effects to enhance photographs.

Photo Editing Effects

Poster Design

Outdoor Hoarding Design

Newspaper Ads Design

Design compelling newspaper advertisements that attract readers' attention.
